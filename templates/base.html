Company Introduction

Influx Global was founded in 2020. It is a subsidiary of America Film-world, specializing in promoting movies through pre-sales, announcements, and other means before their release, making them well-known and ensuring that all members have access to global movie news and can participate in movie promotion and receive investment returns.

Earn Money with Influx Global

Influx Global has partnerships with movie theaters around the world. Users can purchase pre-sale movie tickets and daily movie tickets through the Influx Global application, which helps the movies increase their box office performance, thereby increasing their popularity and success in the market. As a result, the movie's producer pays Influx Global a certain amount of promotion and advertising fees. Users can earn income through Influx Global's loyalty reward program, member level system, and team income plan while supporting the movie industry.
